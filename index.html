About Us

We are a group of startup enthusiast from IIM Bangalore. One fine day during a normal entrepreneurship group activity we realized that there is a need for parking spaces, especially in tier 1 and tier 2 cities where the roads are cramped up with vehicles and no places to park. We ourselves have faced this issue many a times where we had to park our vehicles on roadside despite the fear of getting the vehicle towed away. We had no other option. So we decided to work on a project aimed at solving the issue of unavailable parking space.

We did a project on this under the guidance of Prof Srivardhini Jha, one of the faculty members of IIM Bangalore. And now, a few of us are working on taking it forward as something non-academic and on a bigger level.

Services

We are building a platform where we will be connecting individuals as well as big corporates who need parking space to available parking places near their destination. We aim at doing this by managing to bring apartments, societies and malls on board.

For Individuals

Anyone can come to our website or app and look for any available parking space nearby and can book a slot. You will be charged at an hourly rate. During rush hours, there might be surcharges depending on the location.

For corporates:

We will be providing quote depending on the details (like number of cars and location) you will be sharing with us. We will then make a contract for the smooth future transactions.


Why choose us?


For Individuals:

We are the only service of our kind who is providing you parking spaces anywhere you go. We are providing you tension free time without any worry of parking the vehicle at roadside and getting hit by someone or getting it towed away. That too at a very reasonable cost. We value for your vehicle’s safety and security.

For Businesses

We are providing parking space for your employees with the guarantee of the vehicle’s safety. It is cheap and better option than parking it on the roadside which apart from risks, dampens the company’s image. We ensure smooth transactions and flexibility. Your employees will not have to go search for places to park and waste the time in frustration that they could have used otherwise. We care for you.
